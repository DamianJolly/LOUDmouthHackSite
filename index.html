The game changer

The Large Cable File Bag was designed by gigging musicians. It's our first attempt to change the rules. We’ve experienced firsthand how frustrating it is to forget a vital accessory or lead. We also hate rummaging through a black vortex in the dark looking for something we’ve packed but can’t seem to place our hands on - so we designed CFB to solve these problems.

The Ins and Outs

Why It Works

1

The bright yellow interior makes it easy to find things even in dim lighting.

2

Everything important goes inside the bag. There are no awkward pockets on the outside and no worries about your gear being knocked, damaged or lost.

3

It’s easy to store and stack and will slide in and out of your car or van without getting snagged.

4

The standard 6”x4” index card holder inside the lid will help you keep track of what’s important and the lid pocket is perfect for storing a tablet or notepad.

5

Made from PVC backed 900d polyester and sporting a 10C zipper, the bag is light and tough to protect your gear from the elements
